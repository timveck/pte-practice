Pro Tips for Collocations

Quick start
🔗 Collocation matters

Learn the word with its common partner – e.g. reach a conclusion, not make a conclusion.

✍️ Fill in the Blanks

Preposition collocations (e.g. responsible for, consistent with) are tested frequently in the reading and listening fill‑in‑the‑blanks tasks.

📝 Essay writing

Use 2–3 collocations per paragraph – they make your writing sound natural and improve your vocabulary score.

⚠️ Trap collocations

Avoid common errors – see the Trap Collocations section at the end.
