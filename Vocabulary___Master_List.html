Pro Tips for Vocabulary

Quick start
✏️ Spelling

Spelling matters – practice writing each word 3 times. Common traps: accommodation, environment, committee.

📊 Describe Image

For Describe Image, the words from Group 2 are your "cheat sheet" – use them in every response (fluctuate, peak, soar, plummet).

✍️ Essay

For Essay, use 2–3 Group 1 words and 2–3 connectors from Group 3 per paragraph.

🔗 Collocations

Always learn the word with its common partner – see the Collocations file for 200+ pairs.
